About Adela

Your personal
cooking instructor

Adela Nadira has turned a simple idea into something remarkable: instead of hosting a party where guests sit and wait, she puts them at the center of the experience — aprons on, hands in the dough.

She guides your group through cooking unique dishes from scratch, creating a shared experience that's part class, part celebration, and entirely memorable.

Whether it's a birthday, a casual dinner with friends, or any gathering in between — every event is crafted around your group, your taste, and your occasion.

The experience

What happens
at your party

Each experience is designed to be hands-on, social, and suited to all skill levels. No cooking background needed — just a willingness to learn and have fun.

01

Cooking Party

Your guests become the chefs. Adela guides everyone through a unique dish together — the perfect centerpiece for birthdays, celebrations, and group gatherings of any size.

02

Unique Dishes

Forget the usual party food. Adela introduces recipes that surprise and delight — dishes your guests have never made before, but will want to recreate at home the very next week.

How it works

From your kitchen
to an experience

1

You reach out

Email Adela with your event date, approximate number of guests, and any special requests. She'll reply quickly to discuss the details.

2

You plan together

Choose the recipes, confirm the logistics, and relax. Adela handles everything culinary — ingredients, equipment, and the full session plan.

3

The experience happens

Adela arrives, sets up, and leads your guests through an interactive cooking session. Everyone participates, everyone learns, everyone eats.

4

Your guests leave happy

With new skills, great memories, and full stomachs. It's the kind of party people talk about for weeks.
